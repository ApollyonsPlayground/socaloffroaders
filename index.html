Trail Etiquette

TL
🛤️

Travel Responsibly

Stay on designated trails. Don't create new routes.

🗑️

Pack It Out

Leave no trace. Pack out all trash including TP.

🔥

Minimize Impact

Respect wildlife, historical sites, and other users.

🤝

Be Considerate

Yield to uphill traffic. Help others when safe.

Tread Lightly. Leave it better than you found it.
